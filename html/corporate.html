Corporate Flair

Sometimes content can feel uninspired. Dull, repeated templates are used to communicate hours of corporate compliance. Paragraphs seem to get longer and longer as they try to explain complex concepts. You know you need to learn it- so you drag through it.

To help alleviate brain fog, I incorporated simplicity and imagery into content anywhere I could. Enjoy these fun, supportive side projects I've worked on through my years at Indeed.

Team Logo

Indeedians loved an acronym. At one point in my career, I lead the Communications, Auditing, and Training teams which we delightfully named "CAT". As the Captain of the CATship, I designed a logo for the collective team.

Wonkavision

While supporting the pka Search Quality Operations ("Squall Ops") organization, I took every opportunity to create "ads" to help bridge relationships with teams. These ads were displayed on TVs in offices across the globe.
